Here are four crystals displayed as buttons in front of you. You will see a TARGET NUMBER at the start of the game. When you click on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to the your total score. Your game will hide this amount until you click on a crystal.

You win only if your total score matches the random number from the beginning of the game. If you do, a horse will whinny to congratulate your success. But if you get more point than what you are supposed to, you lose! And you will hear a voice of a cat to share your bad luck!

Whatever you win or lose, the game will automatically restart in a few seconds.

When the game begins again, you should see a new target number. Also, all the crystals will have four new hidden values. Of course, your score will reset to zero.

GOOD LUCK!
